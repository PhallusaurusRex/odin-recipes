PopTarts

Description

It's PopTarts!

Ingredients

  • PopTart
  • Another PopTart (Optional, they are packaged in pairs. Feel free to mix and match! Have fun with it! Woo!

Steps

  1. Open PopTart box(es) if you have not already
  2. Unsheathe PopTart(s) of your choosing from their wrapping
  3. Apply chosen PopTart(s) to toaster or toaster oven
  4. Make hot the PopTart(s)
  5. Let PopTarts cool. Be careful! They may be extremely hot and cause burns!
  6. When temperature of PopTarts becomes comfortably bearable, apply to mouth

Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

It's a peanut butter sandwich. What, you never heard of a peanut butter sandwich?

It's pretty self explanatory

Ingredients

  • Bread (Any kind sliced or sliceable bread you like)
  • Peanuts

    • Butter

Steps

  1. Get that bread
  2. Apply butter to bread
  3. Drizzle peanuts over butter bread
